Who's Behind Visualise NZ

Meet Melissa

Melissa's career began in marketing — a degree, followed by years in marketing communications for corporate organisations across New Zealand, the UK and Australia, developing a sharp instinct for how to present an idea so it actually lands.

That instinct became the foundation for a progressive move into interior design, where she holds a Diploma in Interior Design along with further study in commercial design, kitchen and bathroom design, and Sketchup. It's this combination — genuine design training, backed by a career built on communicating ideas clearly — that shapes every render she produces: never just a pretty picture, always built to help you sell the vision.

Based on the Kāpiti Coast and working with clients throughout New Zealand, Melissa is easy to work with, realistic about pricing, and always keen to hear about the next project. Get in touch — she'd love to help bring your project to life.
